About the Project

Why AI Conversation Analyzer exists, what we stand for, and who's building it.

We believe developers deserve better tools to understand AI conversations.

AI agents are becoming a core part of software development workflows. They write code, run tools, reason through problems, and produce outputs that developers need to trust and debug. But most teams are still staring at raw JSON dumps, trying to make sense of token counts, tool call sequences, and reasoning traces by hand.

AI Conversation Analyzer was built to change that. It takes the raw output from AI agent sessions and transforms it into rich, interactive dashboards — giving developers the visibility they need to understand exactly what happened, why it happened, and how to improve it.

Our Mission

Make every AI conversation transparent.

We're building the definitive tool for inspecting, debugging, and understanding AI agent conversations — and we're doing it as open source.

Visibility

Every reasoning step, tool call, token usage spike, and latency anomaly should be visible to the developer. No black boxes, no guesswork.

Privacy

AI conversations contain sensitive code, business logic, and personal information. That data should never leave your machine. Analysis happens 100% in the browser.

Extensibility

AI platforms evolve fast. The analyzer needs to keep up. A pluggable connector architecture means new platforms can be supported without rewriting the core.

Our Values

What guides us.

Six principles that shape every decision we make about the project.

📖

Transparency

Every part of the analyzer is open for inspection. Our code, our roadmap, our decisions — all public. We believe transparency builds trust, and trust builds community.

🔒

Privacy

Your conversations are yours. No telemetry, no analytics, no cloud processing. The analyzer runs entirely in your browser and makes zero network requests beyond loading its own static assets.

📖

Open Source

MIT licensed, forkable, and community-driven. We believe the best developer tools are built in the open, where anyone can contribute, audit, and improve them.

🛠️

Developer Experience

Zero build steps, zero configuration, zero friction. Drop the files on any static server and start analyzing. The tool should get out of your way and let you focus on understanding your AI conversations.

👥

Community

We don't just accept contributions — we actively seek them out. Every issue, every pull request, every discussion shapes the direction of the project. The community is the project.

🔌

Extensibility

AI platforms come and go. The analyzer's pluggable connector system ensures it can adapt to any format, any platform, any workflow — without core rewrites or vendor dependencies.
